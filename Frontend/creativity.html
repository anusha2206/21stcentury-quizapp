What is Creative Thinking?

Creative thinking is the ability to consider something in a new way or from a different perspective. It involves generating original ideas, finding unique solutions to problems, and approaching challenges with an open and flexible mindset. Creative thinking is not limited to artistic endeavors; it's a valuable skill in every field, from science and technology to business and education.

Innovation builds upon creative thinking by transforming creative ideas into practical solutions that create value. It's the process of implementing creative thoughts to develop new products, services, processes, or ways of thinking that improve existing conditions. In today's rapidly changing world, the ability to think creatively and innovate is essential for personal growth, career success, and contributing meaningfully to society.
